how to be human

a comprehensive guide of some sort... from someone who does not actually feel like they're human? anyway, these are completely normal observations which may be useful when you're pretending to be human.
there is no assurance that they won't come and get you even if you successfully followed each tip. good luck!
reactions with a crowd

never forget that humans are social animals. if something unfortunate happens, mimic the way they look pitifully at the object. I made a mistake on bursting into excitement when a classmate of mine fainted--- questionable stares soon followed.

  
how conversations work

do not try to talk to people unless you've figured what their background is. it is better knowing what they like before striking up an open-ended remark. nonetheless, there will always be people who'll look at you like you're worthless--- ignore it. remember, we're better than them.
